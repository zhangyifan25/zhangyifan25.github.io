Yifan Zhang

Yifan Zhang Ph.D. student at Beihang University, supervised by Prof. Zhiguo Jiang and Associate Prof. Haopeng Zhang. My research interests include deep learning, remote sensing image interpretation, weakly supervised semantic segmentation, and zero-shot self-supervised denoising. I am currently actively seeking academic exchanges and collaboration opportunities. Welcome interested researchers and students to contact me!

We actively recruit Post-doc Fellows, Ph.D./Master students, and Research Interns! Welcome to contact me with your detailed CV!
